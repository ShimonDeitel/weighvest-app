Privacy Policy

Weighvest is an offline-first app. All data you enter is stored locally on your device in the app's private storage. We do not collect, transmit, or sell any personal data. The app makes no network requests except to Apple's App Store servers for subscription purchase verification via StoreKit.

We do not use analytics, advertising identifiers, or third-party trackers.

If you delete the app, your local data is deleted with it.
